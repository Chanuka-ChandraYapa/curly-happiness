About

A practice built on
careful reasoning.

I believe the law is most powerful when it is clearly understood — by the people it protects, as much as by those who practise it.

With over a decade of experience in commercial and constitutional law, I have represented clients ranging from individual claimants to publicly listed corporations before the Supreme Court of Sri Lanka, the Court of Appeal, and in international arbitration proceedings.

My approach is direct: I take time to understand the full picture before I advise, and I communicate my thinking plainly so clients can make informed decisions at every stage.

Before entering private practice, I read law at the University of Colombo and completed an LLM at Cambridge. I am currently a member of the Bar Association of Sri Lanka and serve on the Advisory Committee for Legal Aid.
